Airsoft battleground

GROUND EVENT #2

Experience the real life PubG.

Date : February 07, 2019

Prizes Worth : 6k

Registration:500/Team(3 members)

Love PUBG? How about playing something similar in real life?
Work together as a squad, shoot down your opponents and rise to the top.

The game involves teams trying to shoot out the balloons on opponents within a time limit. Each team member can choose one of four roles and the corresponding weapon will be provided.
You will be in an arena with obstacles, plants, vehicles, etc. as cover. The team which manages to shoot out the maximum number of the opponentsâ€™ balloons within the time limit will win the round.
The game will be quite fast and high energy. You are encouraged to continuously move around. The arena is designed in a way to discourage people from sitting in one position alone.

Rules and Regulations

Each team shall have a maximum of four members.
As a squad you may dress up in any uniforms or costumes if you wish to do so. Custom masks are also allowed.
Each person has three lives (balloons) at the start more can be picked up on the arena.
You are allowed to change roles between rounds
No physical contact (melee) is allowed. The team will be disqualified from the round if such issues are created.
The event is open to both men and women of all ages.
Once your lives are over you have to leave the arena for the round. If you continue playing one of your team members (one with maximum life will be asked to leave the round).

The rules on the final day may vary a little bit from the above-mentioned ones to improve the game, and the event organizers reserve the right to change them. However, we will ensure that they remain fair to everyone

Disclaimer : We are not responsible for any physical injuries during game play.

GENERAL EVENT #3

Line Follower

Date : February 08, 2019

Registration:100/team

Prize Worth : 3k

Venue : Multipurpose Building

Rules and Regulations

Winner will be determined by the minimum time taken to complete the line path by the bot.
The line to be traced (black line) is of 30mm in width.
Teams are not allowed to replace parts between runs. (but with a penality defective sensors can be replaced)
Change of batteries and modifications fo the code are allowed but the game play has to be restarted
If the robot is manually touched except at the start,then it will be considered as a violation and will lead to penality.

Bot Specs

The maximum dimensions of the base of the bot should be *25cmx25cmx25cm*
Robots must not use ready-made mechanisms, Lego kits, etc.
